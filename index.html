About Us

The Sovereign Way is a Not For Profit Company, dedicated to providing curated information and technological resources that increase freedom and human rights globally. Starting local, but aiming big. Using recycled ewaste, we strive to help those that struggle to afford keeping up with todays digital challenges.

Everyone can be Sovereign

We belive that the world is at a point of inflection. Old systems that have not been serving us are in decline. The future is not yet set. But failure to act when the option is available to us can lead to oportunities lost. In a world where data is the new gold, and AI promises to know us better than we know ourselves, we believe that the ability to choose who you intereact with and where data about you is stored is imperative to remaining sovereign.

What does it mean to be Sovereign? To us, it means having the ability to make choices about your life. Where you can live, what you can eat, how you can spend your wealth. Some of these freedoms are under threat already. We aim to help individuals empower themselves so that they have as many options open to them.

Founder and CEO, Harley Linton, is a passionate father of 2, a Technologist, Entrepreneur, Nature Lover and Freedom advocate.
